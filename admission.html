Official Enrollment for Freshmen and Transferees

Requirements:

PSA Birth Certificate
Form 138 (Report Card)

Onsite

Step 1 Fill out your enrollment form at the Marketing Office.
Step 2 Pay the enrollment fee: ₱1,000.00
Step 3 Get your ORF (Official Registration Form) or Class schedule at the assessment area.
Step 4 Go back to the Marketing Office for orientation and get your welcome kit.

Online

Step 1 Fill out the online enrollment form.
Step 2 Check your email if you received a notification of your successful registration receipt.
Step 3 Wait for our team to call you and guide you on to the next process.
Step 4 Check email for your class schedule or ORF (Official Registration Form).
Register Here

Onsite (Transferees)

Step 1 Go to the Dean’s Office (See Dean or Program Head) for subject crediting with the following requirements: (TOR, Birth Certificate, Honorable Dismissal.)


Step 2 Go to the Finance Office to pay the enrollment fee – ₱1,000.00
Step 3 Go to the Registrar Office for Enlistment and Printing of Official Registration Form (ORF) or in case the schedule is not yet ready.
